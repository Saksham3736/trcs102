## What is Agentic AI?

Agentic AI refers to AI systems capable of:

- Planning
- Reasoning
- Making decisions
- Using tools
- Executing tasks autonomously

Unlike traditional AI, Agentic AI performs multiple actions to achieve a goal instead of simply responding to user prompts.
